Summary of Chateaus Rooms

Old European Boutique Chateaus and each with a Private Screened in Gazebo, Walkout Balcony and Outdoor Hot Tub to soothe life's pains away while enjoying views of spectacular scenery overlooking sparkling Galeairy Lake onto the romantic forests of Algonquin Park.

Travel the World and one will quickly see that it does not get any better than this anywhere.

In all 4 seasons, explore the stars from your private Outdoor Hot Tub located on your deck.

Towering 16 foot vaulted ceiling and a massive 2 sided stone fireplace between the bedroom and living room.

Furnished by Canada's finest furniture store The Art Shoppe of Toronto.
