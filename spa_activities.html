Algonquin Park: A Paradise at Your Doorstep

Step outside your luxurious accommodation and into the awe-inspiring embrace of Algonquin Park. This world-renowned park, a haven for diverse wildlife and stunning natural landscapes, awaits your exploration.

Read More About Algonquin Park...

Activities

Discover the park's rich geological history, shaped by glaciers ten thousand years ago.

Hike through majestic forests and canoe across pristine lakes, and spot iconic wildlife like moose and loons.

Immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature, just steps away from Couples Resort.

Spa: Rejuvenate Your Mind, Body, and Spirit

Indulge in a world of pampering at our award-winning spa. Our expert therapists offer a variety of treatments designed to soothe your muscles, revitalize your skin, and melt away stress.

Discover Complete Spa Menu...

Activities

Explore our menu of massages, including hot stone therapy, Indian head massage, and body wraps.

Treat yourself to a manicure, pedicure, or facial for a touch of ultimate relaxation.

Unwind in our fitness room, sauna, or outdoor heated saltwater pool after your treatment.

Spring Activities: Embrace the Rejuvenation of the Season

Spring paints Algonquin Park in vibrant hues, making it the perfect time for a romantic getaway. Couples Resort offers a variety of activities to reconnect with nature and each other.

Explore Spring Packages...

Activities

Hike through scenic trails with wildflowers or explore the hidden coves of Galeairy Lake by canoe or kayak.

Go horseback riding through the wilderness or enjoy a refreshing swim in our heated pool.

Savor delicious gourmet meals and cozy up by the fireplace in your private cabin after a day of adventure.

Summer Activities: Endless Adventure Awaits

Summer at Couples Resort is all about creating unforgettable memories with your loved one. With endless activities and breathtaking scenery, every day is an opportunity for exploration and connection.

Unveil Summer Packages...

Activities

Spend your days canoeing, kayaking, or fishing on the lake, surrounded by the tranquillity of nature.

Explore miles of scenic biking trails or go for a refreshing swim in our outdoor pool.

Play tennis, pickleball, or badminton on our courts, or simply relax on your private deck with a good book.

Fall Activities: Witness Nature's Dazzling Transformation

Fall transforms Algonquin Park into a masterpiece of color, making it a breathtaking backdrop for a romantic escape. Couples Resort offers the perfect haven to experience the beauty of the season.

Explore Fall Packages...

Activities

Witness the awe-inspiring fall foliage, with sugar maples and red oaks painting the landscape in vibrant hues.

Embark on a wildlife viewing adventure and spot majestic moose or listen to the haunting calls of wolves.

Take a scenic boat tour on Galeairy Lake or explore local artisan studios to discover handcrafted treasures.

Winter Activities: A Winter Wonderland for Couples

Winter at Couples Resort offers a magical escape, where romance and adventure intertwine amidst a snowy wonderland.

Discover Winter Packages...

Activities

Cozy up by the fireplace in your private cabin and savor delicious gourmet meals after a day of winter fun.
